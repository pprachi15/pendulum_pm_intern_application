The Real Challenge

Where Analytics Platforms Lose Their Users

Pendulum Intelligence is building next-gen analytics infrastructure. But powerful data tools fail when the product doesn't translate insight into action. Here's where the gap usually lives.

📊

Dashboards Built for Builders, Not Users

Analysts and business leaders think differently. Most analytics platforms optimize for the engineer who built them, not the executive who needs a decision in 5 minutes. Complexity leaks into the interface.

🔁

Features Shipped Without Customer Signal

LLM-powered features get built based on what's technically possible, not what customers actually struggle with. Discovery and delivery stay disconnected, leading to low adoption post-launch.

⚡

Alerting and Reporting Workflows That Don't Scale

Ad hoc requests pile up. Report scheduling becomes a bottleneck. Without scalable automation baked into the product from day one, analysts spend more time pulling data than acting on it.

🎯

GTM and Product Misaligned on Packaging

Analytics platforms frequently misjudge packaging and pricing because PM and GTM operate in silos. Features get buried in tiers customers don't buy, or bundled in ways that confuse buyers.
